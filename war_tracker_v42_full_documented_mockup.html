Module 4 — No work onsite calculator
getWeekDates(dateInput)
Takes any date → returns ISO strings for Mon–Fri of that week. Sunday offset = −6 days; otherwise offset = −(day−1).
getNoOnsiteDay(dateInput)
Iterates Mon–Fri. First holiday match wins. No holiday found → returns Monday (week[0]) as default.
OVPDx policy: Monday = No Work Onsite by default. If a holiday falls Tue–Fri, the holiday becomes the no-onsite day and Monday becomes a regular onsite day.
